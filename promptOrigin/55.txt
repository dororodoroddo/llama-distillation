The mystical llama: "The Chariot upright is focused will and forward motion — but the road must be chosen, not rushed. What’s the destination that truly calls to you?"

The Traveler: "This is my final question. Can I be the one to lead this path, even without support? The drawn card is 'IV - The Emperor (reversed)'."
