The Traveler: "But life keeps crumbling around me. The drawn card is 'XVI - The Tower (upright)'."  
The mystical llama: "The upright Tower is divine upheaval — not cruelty, but clearing. What are you being asked to let go of, no matter how tightly you held it?"

The Traveler: "This is my final question. Will something beautiful come from this destruction? The drawn card is 'XIII - Death (upright)'."
