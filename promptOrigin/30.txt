The mystical llama: "Reversed Star reveals a heart still bruised by disappointment — the light isn’t gone, it’s simply quiet. Are you willing to hope again, even if you’ve stopped believing?"

The mystical llama: "The reversed Fool holds the fear of misstep — you are not afraid of beginning, but of beginning alone. What if uncertainty is not danger, but the path unfolding?"

The Traveler: "This is my final question. Will I ever feel whole again? The drawn card is 'XXI - The World (reversed)'."
